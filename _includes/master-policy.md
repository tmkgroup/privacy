### Who we are

**TMK Group** publishes privacy-first mobile and web applications. Each
app on this domain has its own data-practices section above; this
general statement applies on top of it.

- **Publisher**: TMK Group
- **Contact**: [dev@tmkgroup.vn](mailto:dev@tmkgroup.vn)
- **Domain**: [tmkgroup.vn](https://tmkgroup.vn)

### Our principles

1. **No selling, no sharing, no advertising profile**. We never sell
   your personal data, never share it with advertisers, and never build
   a profile of you for marketing.
2. **Minimum data possible**. Each app collects only what it strictly
   needs to function. Most of our apps collect nothing at all.
3. **On-device first**. Where possible, all processing and storage
   happens locally on your device. We design for airplane-mode use.
4. **No hidden third parties**. The "Third-party services" section of
   each app lists every external service it talks to. If we add one,
   the policy is updated and the app version is bumped.
5. **Transparency by version**. Every change to this policy is tracked
   in [the public Git history of the privacy
   repository](https://github.com/tmkgroup/privacy). You can see when
   any wording changed and why.

### Your rights (GDPR, CCPA, and similar regimes)

Even when we hold no personal data about you, you always have the
right to:

- **Access** any data an app stores (use the in-app export where
  available — most of our apps store everything in plain on-device
  files you already control).
- **Erase** any data — uninstall the app or use the in-app reset, and
  the data is gone with it.
- **Object / restrict / port** your data — same as above.
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority.

### Children's privacy

Our apps are suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly
collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in
the EU). Most of our apps collect nothing from anyone.

### International users

Our apps are distributed globally through Google Play and the Apple App
Store. Where any data is processed (rare), it is processed on-device,
in the user's own jurisdiction. We do not transfer personal data
internationally because, in most apps, we do not hold personal data at
all.

### Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we update the "Last updated" date on the
app-specific page above and ship a new app version. Since most of our
apps collect no data, any changes to this general statement only
clarify or expand your rights.
